The Let’s Read! platform makes digital storybooks available in national and indigenous languages.

Let’s Read! is an initiative of The Asia Foundation’s Books for Asia program that fosters young readers in Asia. This library gathers high-quality children’s digital stories and makes them available in national and indigenous languages.

 

We focus on underserved languages, seek engaging, original content from within communities and support community workshops that enable individuals to collaborate together on creating culturally relevant stories.

 

Learn more about the Let’s Read! initiative: www.booksforasia.org
